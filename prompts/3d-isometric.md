# 3D & Isometric Prompts / 3D 与等距视角提示词

> 3D renders, isometric views, and product visualization prompts for Midjourney V7.

---

### Prompt 1: Futuristic Vehicle 3D
**Parameters:** `--ar 16:9 --v 7`
**Prompt:**
```
3D-Illustration of a futuristic vehicle with smooth surfaces and dynamic lines, illuminated under a starry sky, octane render, volumetric lighting --ar 16:9 --v 7
```
**Best for:** Product concept renders, sci-fi vehicle design, portfolio-ready 3D illustrations.
**Tips:** Starting with "3D-Illustration" sets the rendering paradigm immediately. "Octane render" and "volumetric lighting" are 3D industry terms that push Midjourney toward professional CG output with realistic light behavior. The starry sky backdrop creates natural specular highlights on the vehicle's smooth surfaces.

### Prompt 2: Isometric City Map
**Parameters:** `--ar 16:9 --v 7`
**Prompt:**
```
Create an isometric representation of a complex, futuristic city map in which all buildings and streets are in perfect harmony with each other and visible from a bird's eye view --ar 16:9 --v 7
```
**Best for:** Game level design, urban planning concepts, world-building visualizations.
**Tips:** The phrase "perfect harmony with each other" instructs Midjourney to produce a cohesive layout rather than random buildings. "Bird's eye view" combined with "isometric" ensures the correct camera angle. The 16:9 ratio gives enough horizontal space for a sprawling cityscape.

### Prompt 3: Pixel Art Fantasy
**Parameters:** `--ar 16:9 --v 7`
**Prompt:**
```
Fantasy game, 16-bit pixel art --ar 16:9 --v 7
```
**Best for:** Retro game asset inspiration, indie game mood boards, nostalgic pixel art studies.
**Tips:** Extreme brevity works well here because "16-bit pixel art" is a highly specific visual style that V7 interprets precisely. Adding too many descriptors can dilute the pixel art aesthetic. Let Midjourney fill in the fantasy details based on its training data for SNES-era game art.

### Prompt 4: Cute Isometric Room
**Parameters:** `--ar 1:1 --s 500 --v 7`
**Prompt:**
```
Isometric cute miniature bedroom, pastel colors, kawaii aesthetic, cozy tiny furniture, soft shadows, clay render style --ar 1:1 --s 500 --v 7
```
**Best for:** Cozy interior illustrations, kawaii-themed artwork, miniature diorama concepts.
**Tips:** The high `--s 500` stylize value enhances the artistic charm and softness. "Clay render style" gives objects a matte, tactile quality that pairs perfectly with the kawaii aesthetic. "Soft shadows" prevents harsh lighting that would break the gentle mood. The 1:1 square ratio suits the diorama-like framing.

### Prompt 5: Product Exploded View
**Parameters:** `--ar 1:1 --s 150 --v 7`
**Prompt:**
```
Exploded view of a running shoe, all components separated and floating, technical diagram style, pure white background, ultra-detailed, commercial product photography --ar 1:1 --s 150 --v 7
```
**Best for:** Product design presentations, technical illustration, e-commerce marketing assets.
**Tips:** A moderate `--s 150` keeps the result clean and diagrammatic rather than overly artistic. "Exploded view" is a precise technical term that V7 understands as components spread apart in space. "Pure white background" ensures a professional, studio-quality result ideal for commercial use. The 1:1 ratio works well for product catalogs and social media.
