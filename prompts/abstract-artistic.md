# Abstract & Artistic Prompts / 抽象与艺术提示词

> Abstract art, experimental aesthetics, and artistic expression prompts for Midjourney V7. Higher stylize values work best in this category.

---

## Fluid & Dynamic

### Prompt 1: Metallic Paint Collision
**Parameters:** --ar 1:1 --s 600 --c 30 --v 7
**Prompt:**
```
Two streams of metallic paint colliding in mid-air, gold and teal, frozen in time, high-speed photography style, black background, abstract art
```
**Best for:** Abstract backgrounds, title sequences, artistic content
**Tips:** High chaos (30) creates unique variations. The high stylize (600) gives the AI more artistic freedom with the fluid dynamics.

### Prompt 2: Geometric Light Refraction

Geometric light refraction through crystal prisms, creating rainbow spectrum patterns, top-down view, multiple light sources, album cover art

### Prompt 3: Ink in Water Galaxy
**Parameters:** --ar 1:1 --s 800 --c 40 --v 7
**Prompt:**
```
Macro photography of colored ink drops expanding in water, creating galaxy-like formations, deep purple and gold, cosmic aesthetic, ethereal, dreamlike
```
**Best for:** Backgrounds, wallpapers, album art, meditation visuals
**Tips:** Very high stylize (800) + high chaos (40) produces dramatically different results each time.

## Painterly & Textural

### Prompt 4: Impressionist Garden
**Parameters:** --ar 16:9 --s 900 --v 7
**Prompt:**
```
A garden in full bloom painted in thick impasto brushstrokes, Claude Monet inspired, vibrant splashes of color, visible canvas texture, dappled light
```
**Best for:** Fine art prints, wall art, artistic inspiration
**Tips:** Stylize at 900 pushes the painterly quality to maximum. Named artist reference (Monet) guides the style direction.
