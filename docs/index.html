The 6-Part Midjourney V7 Prompt Formula

Every effective V7 prompt follows this structure. V7 processes prompts sequentially -- words at the beginning carry significantly more weight.

Subject + Action + Environment + Lighting + Style + Parameters
1

Subject

The main focus. Be specific. Goes FIRST -- most weight.

2

Action

What is happening? Adds dynamism and narrative.

3

Environment

Where? Provides context, foreground and background.

4

Lighting

3x better results when specified. Golden hour, side light, etc.

5

Style

Visual treatment: photorealistic, cinematic, anime, oil painting.

6

Parameters

Technical settings: --ar, --s, --c, --v 7. Always at the END.

Complete Example

a woman in a flowing emerald dress dancing on a rooftop at sunset,
warm golden hour light creating long shadows, city skyline silhouette,
cinematic, shot on Arri Alexa, film grain
--ar 16:9 --s 350 --c 10 --v 7

Prompt Engineering Tips

Based on analysis of 1000+ V7 generations. These tips will immediately improve your results.

▶ Front-load Important Details

V7 weighs early words more heavily. Put your subject first, not buried in the middle.

BAD:  shot on film, photorealistic, woman in red dress
GOOD: woman in red dress, photorealistic, shot on film

▶ Natural Language Over Keywords

V7 understands full sentences better than comma-separated keywords.

BAD:  forest, sunlight, morning, canopy, filter, rays
GOOD: morning sunlight filtering through forest canopy

▶ Always Specify Lighting

Omitting lighting produces approximately 3x worse results. Any specific lighting descriptor beats none.

golden hour | side lighting | soft window light
dramatic rim lighting | volumetric beams

▶ One Main Subject Per Prompt

Multiple equal subjects confuse V7. Stick to one primary focus per generation.

WORKS:     luxury watch on marble
STRUGGLES: luxury watch, smartphone, and laptop on marble

▶ Match Chaos to Your Goal

Wrong chaos values waste GPU time. Use low chaos for consistency, high for exploration.

Product photos: --c 0
Portraits:      --c 0-15
Creative:       --c 20-40
Abstract art:   --c 40-100

▶ Keep Prompts Focused

More words does not mean better results. Edit ruthlessly. If a detail does not serve the main idea, cut it.

BAD:  drone through misty forest at dawn with golden
      light rays while birds fly and deer graze...
GOOD: FPV drone through misty redwood forest at dawn,
      golden god rays --ar 16:9 --s 300 --v 7

What's New in Midjourney V7

Released April 3, 2025. Default model since June 17, 2025. A ground-up rework focused on photorealism, coherence, and prompt precision.

NEW

Video Generation

First Midjourney model to support video. Up to 21 seconds with smooth cinematic camera movements. Use keywords like FPV flight, tracking shot, orbital, crane down, push-in.

NEW

Draft Mode

10x faster generation at half the GPU cost. Lower resolution for rapid prototyping. Upscale and enhance later. Perfect for iterating before committing to full-quality renders.

NEW

Conversational Mode

Interactive chat-like prompting. Say "make it winter" and it updates the scene while keeping the composition. Voice input supported for iterative refinement.

NEW

Omni Reference (--oref)

Replaces V6's --cref. Now supports characters, objects, vehicles, and creatures (not just human faces). Use --ow to control reference weight. Works with --p, --s, --sref.

NEW

Experimental Parameter (--exp)

Range 0-100. Adds advanced tone mapping and detail enhancement. Best kept under 25 for consistent results. Over 25 produces unpredictable but creative outputs.

IMPROVED

Better Photorealism

Significantly improved skin textures, fabric details, light and shadow, and composition. Fewer hand and finger artifacts. Expanded stylize range: 0-1000 (was 0-750 in V6).

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Midjourney V7 prompts, parameters, and best practices.

What is Midjourney V7? ▼

Midjourney V7 is the latest version of the AI image generation model by Midjourney, released April 2025 and default since June 2025. It features significantly improved photorealism, video generation up to 21 seconds, a new Omni Reference (--oref) system, Draft Mode (10x faster), Conversational Mode, and better natural language understanding compared to V6. It is described as a "ground-up rework" focused on photographic realism, physical coherence, and precise prompt interpretation.

How do I write a good Midjourney V7 prompt? ▼

Follow the 6-part formula: Subject + Action + Environment + Lighting + Style + Parameters. Put your subject first (V7 weighs early words more heavily), specify lighting for 3x better results, and keep parameters at the end. Use natural language instead of keyword stuffing. Stick to one main subject per prompt. Front-load the most important details.

What parameters are new in Midjourney V7? ▼

V7 introduces four new parameters: --oref (Omni Reference, replaces --cref -- now supports characters, objects, vehicles, and creatures), --ow (Omni Weight, community recommends ~400), --exp (experimental aesthetics, range 0-100, best under 25), and --q 4 (maximum quality tier). Personalization (--p) is also on by default for the first time.

What is the best --s (stylize) value for Midjourney V7? ▼

It depends on your use case: Product photography: --s 100-200. Portraits: --s 250-350. Landscapes: --s 250-400. Cinematic shots: --s 300-400. Abstract art: --s 500-800. Most general use cases work best at 250-400. Start there and adjust. If results look too "AI-ish", lower stylize to 150-250. If results are boring, raise to 350-500.

Midjourney V7 vs V6 -- what changed? ▼

V7 has better photorealism with improved skin textures, fabric details, and fewer hand/finger artifacts. New features include video generation (up to 21 seconds), Omni Reference (--oref), Draft Mode (10x faster, half GPU cost), Conversational Mode, and an expanded stylize range (0-1000 vs 0-750 in V6). V7 also understands natural language significantly better. Old V6 prompts may produce different results in V7 and should be retested.
